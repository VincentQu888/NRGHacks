Abstract

NRGHacks is a single day, interschool, hackathon style event, hosted at Dr. G.W. Williams Secondary School on Friday November 24th 2023 from 12:30pm to 8:30pm.

Events

NRGHacks will have multiple events running parallel to project development. Alongside workshops for students to learn web development, game design, and design process, students will work in groups of 5 or less people to develop a project relating to the hackathon theme.

Details

A hackathon is a coding competition where participants solve real world coding problems. The event will be held in the library of Dr. G.W. Williams S.S. located at 39 Dunning Ave, Aurora, ON L4G 1A2 and will be split into skill groups of Junior and Senior. There is an entrance fee as well.

Divisions

2nd: 15$

Senior

1st: 50$ + NHS & GWW Announcements

2nd: 25$

3rd: 25$

Division Seperation

While the junior division is open to anyone, coders who meet the following standards are recommended to join the senior division:

  • 3 months or more experience in web development
  • 1 year or more experience in programming
  • Proficient in any programming language (Java, C++, Python, Javascript, etc)
  • Grade 11 or 12
  • Or, if you simply are up for a challenge
